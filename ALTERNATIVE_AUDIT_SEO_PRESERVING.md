## STRATEGIC SHIFT

Instead of **removing** "insurance" language entirely, **reframe it** around:

1. ✅ **Our documentation HELPS the insurance process** (doesn't replace it)
2. ✅ **Adjusters and carriers rely on professional contractor assessments** (position as essential tool)
3. ✅ **Homeowners are asked for contractor quotes/assessments** (reframe what we provide)
4. ✅ **Keep insurance SEO keywords** (search intent preserved)
5. ✅ **Legal protection via transparency** (adjuster makes final call, not us)

---

## KEY MESSAGE FRAMEWORK (ALTERNATIVE)

**Instead of:**
- "We provide professional documentation" (vague, loses SEO)
- "Insurance claim help" (claims handler implication)

**Use:**
- "Professional contractor assessment and documentation that insurance adjusters rely on"
- "Expert contractor quotes and scope reports for insurance submission"
- "Professional documentation that helps adjusters and carriers make thorough decisions"
- "Detailed inspection and contractor assessment—what insurance companies request from homeowners"
